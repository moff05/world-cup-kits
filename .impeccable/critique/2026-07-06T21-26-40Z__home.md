## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment:** Not immediately AI-obvious but two tells compound: system fonts on a "cinematic" brand, and uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section break on every page. Kit view layout (photo left, 500px black void right) is the most telling sign of an unfinished layout, not restraint.

**Deterministic scan:** detect.mjs returned 0 findings across src/. No gradient text, no ghost cards, no glassmorphism, no stripe backgrounds.

## Overall Impression

Strong concept, good palette, excellent content. But the kit view wastes half the viewport at desktop widths and the typography has zero voice. A site with a strong idea and a placeholder shell.

## What's Working

1. **Palette** — #080808 + #c9a84c is a committed, non-generic choice. Historical, authoritative.
2. **Annotation concept** — Big photo + interactive gold dots + prose stories is exactly right for this content type.
3. **Content quality** — Writing punches through the minimal UI. "The Greatest Team to Never Win" visible in year grid creates genuine curiosity.

## Priority Issues

**[P1] Empty half-page on kit view at desktop**
- Kit panel is max-width 680px left-aligned inside 1000px container. At 1493px viewport, ~500px of pure black to the right.
- Why: Site success metric is serial reading across years. Layout actively fights this.
- Fix: Use the right half — prev/next year navigation, related kits, or era context sidebar.
- Command: $impeccable layout kit view

**[P1] System font stack has no typographic voice**
- font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI" across entire site.
- Why: "Cinematic. Cultural. Precise." requires editorial type. System fonts communicate nothing.
- Fix: One editorial display typeface for headlines. Neue Haas Grotesk, Aktiv Grotesk, or GT Super. Not Inter or Playfair.
- Command: $impeccable typeset

**[P2] Uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section**
- CONMEBOL, KIT TRADITIONS, WORLD CUP HISTORY, ONE NAME ONLY, FIVE STARS — all 11px/2px letter-spacing.
- Why: Banned pattern. Applied to every structural break, it's AI grammar not brand voice.
- Fix: Reserve label style for one role only. Drop all-caps+tracking inside Kit Traditions fact items.
- Command: $impeccable layout

**[P2] No year navigation inside kit view**
- Brazil 1970 → Brazil 1966 requires: back → scan 23-tile grid → click. 2–3 taps per transition.
- Why: Kills reading momentum; fights the serial-reading UX goal.
- Fix: Prev/next year arrows on kit view, scoped to years with kit data.
- Command: $impeccable craft year navigation

**[P2] Annotation dots not discoverable pre-engagement**
- 14px gold dots, no entrance animation, only hint text below image.
- Why: Mobile visitors likely miss the dots on quick scroll. Null-image list view gives no preview of interaction.
- Fix: Gentle pulse animation on first load, or dot-count badge on photo frame corner.
- Command: $impeccable delight
