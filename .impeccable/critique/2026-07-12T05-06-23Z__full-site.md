## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment:** Not AI-obvious. Committed near-black + gold palette, diagonal clip-paths, and skewY accent line show genuine design intent. Font upgrade (Barlow Condensed + Barlow) removed the system-font tell. Surviving issue: `text-transform: uppercase` + wide `letter-spacing` appears on 11 separate selectors — every structural break uses the same 9–11px tracked uppercase treatment, which is the AI grammar pattern the brand register bans.

**Deterministic scan:** `detect.mjs` returned 0 findings across `src/`. No gradient text, ghost cards, glassmorphism, stripe backgrounds, or sketchy SVGs.

## Overall Impression

Strong concept, committed palette, excellent content. The diagonal geometry gives it a graphic signature. Surviving problems: animation bug that gates stats at opacity 0 with no reduced-motion fallback, and uppercase tracking applied to every structural level of the UI.

## What's Working

1. **Palette discipline** — `#080808` + `#c9a84c` is historically authoritative and non-generic.
2. **Kit view layout** — sticky sidebar year list solves the old empty-viewport P1. Serial reading now feels natural.
3. **Diagonal geometry** — clip-path on image wrap and skewY on home accent rule give the site a specific graphic signature.

## Priority Issues

**[P1] Stat animations gate content at opacity: 0 with no reduced-motion fallback**
- `.country-stat` and `.ext-stat` start at `opacity: 0; transform: translateY(12px)` — invisible until IntersectionObserver fires `.country-stats--on`. No prefers-reduced-motion override.
- Fix: Default to `opacity: 1`. Wrap entrance animation in `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference)`.

**[P2] Uppercase tracked eyebrow on every structural break**
- 11 selectors share the same tracked uppercase treatment: section labels, back links, stat labels, sidebar labels, match round cells, pipeline counter. Applied universally it signals nothing.
- Fix: Reserve tracked uppercase for `.section-label` dividers only. Convert back-links to sentence-case; drop uppercase from pipeline-counter and sidebar labels.

**[P2] Tournament year heading exceeds size ceiling by 67%**
- `.tournament-title: clamp(80px, 16vw, 160px)`. Home h1: `clamp(72px, 13vw, 132px)`. Both exceed 6rem/96px ceiling.
- Fix: `clamp(56px, 10vw, 96px)` home; `clamp(72px, 14vw, 120px)` tournament.

**[P2] Three incompatible year-browsing UIs**
- Country dashboard: accordion. Kit view: sticky sidebar list. Home/tournament: pill strip.
- Fix: Unify the visual vocabulary — same year link styling with gold active state across all contexts.

**[P3] Body type (Barlow) lacks voice**
- Barlow Condensed at 800 works for display. Barlow regular body is indistinguishable from Inter.
- Fix: Swap body to a humanist grotesque with texture. Figtree or Mona Sans would pair well with Condensed display.
